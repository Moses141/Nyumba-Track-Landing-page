The Challenge

Managing Rentals Shouldn't Be This Hard

Every month, thousands of landlords in Uganda struggle with the same frustrating problems. Sound familiar?

Scattered Records

Notebooks, receipts, and spreadsheets everywhere. Finding a tenant's payment history takes hours, not seconds.

Late Payments

Without clear tracking, rent slips through the cracks. You forget who owes what, and tenants take advantage.

Tenant Disputes

"I already paid!" Without digital records, it's your word against theirs. Disputes damage relationships and cost money.

Room Confusion

Which rooms are occupied? Which are vacant? Who's in Room B-3? Manual systems make simple questions complicated.

The Solution

One Simple System for Everything

Nyumba Track brings all your property management into one clear, easy-to-use digital system. No technical skills needed. No expensive enterprise software. Just simple tools that work the way you do.

  • Room-Level Control: Add, activate, block, or assign any room with a few clicks
  • Payment Clarity: See exactly who has paid, who owes, and how much—instantly
  • Dispute-Proof Records: Digital receipts and history that protect both you and your tenants
  • Affordable Pricing: Built for small landlords, not corporate budgets
